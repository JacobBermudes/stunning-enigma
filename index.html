Last updated: April 2026

SurfBoost is built on a strict no-logs policy. When you use our VPN service, we do not monitor, record, log, store, or pass to any third party your internet activities. We do not store your browsing history, connection timestamps, session durations, or original IP addresses.

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal data we collect is used solely to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may use third-party service providers (like payment processors) only to facilitate our services. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
